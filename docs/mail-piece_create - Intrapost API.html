Function: mail-piece/create

Link: https://api.intrapost.nl/mail-piece/create

Description

Register a mail piece

Request:

* = required

Supported content types:
application/json: example
application/xml: example

  • ApiKey*: API key that you can find on the documentation page
  • AccountNumber*: customer accountnumber to be used
  • Product*: service product
    Supported values:
    • [0] Standard: Regular mail 'Standard'
    • [3] FixedDays: Regular mail 'Fixed'
  • WeightGrams: weight in grams (has to be in range between 1 and 2000)
  • Reference: reference, personal input (has to be at most 128 characters long)
  • LabelReference1: reference, will be shown on label (has to be at most 128 characters long)
  • LabelReference2: reference, will be shown on label (has to be at most 128 characters long)
  • OrderReference: reference to combine orders (has to be at most 2048 characters long)
  • ReceiverAddress*: address info of the receiver
    • Street*: street (has to be at most 150 characters long)
    • Number: house/P.O. box number (has to be in range between 1 and 999999)
    • Addition: housenumber addition (has to be at most 15 characters long)
    • Zipcode*: zipcode (has to be at most 15 characters long)
    • City*: city (has to be at most 100 characters long)
    • CountryCode*: 2-letter ISO countrycode (has to be 2 characters long)
    • Company: company name (has to be at most 150 characters long)
    • FullName*: full name (has to be at most 150 characters long)
    • Email: email address (has to be at most 150 characters long)
    • Phone: phonenumber (has to be at most 20 characters long)
  • ReturnAddress: return address info
    • Street*: street (has to be at most 150 characters long)
    • Number: house/P.O. box number (has to be in range between 1 and 999999)
    • Addition: housenumber addition (has to be at most 15 characters long)
    • Zipcode*: zipcode (has to be at most 15 characters long)
    • City*: city (has to be at most 100 characters long)
    • CountryCode*: 2-letter ISO countrycode (has to be 2 characters long)
    • Company: company name (has to be at most 150 characters long)
    • FullName*: full name (has to be at most 150 characters long)
    • Email: email address (has to be at most 150 characters long)
    • Phone: phonenumber (has to be at most 20 characters long)
  • LabelFormatType*: Label type and format to be returned

    • [2] Zpl_Zebra_150x100: ZPL format for the Zebra printer with 150x100 mm labels
